SyriaCoin Roadmap

🚀 Phase 1: Points System

Launch the SyriaCoin points and rewards system to build the first community and encourage participation.

📱 Phase 2: Platform Development

Improve the user experience, develop new features, and create tools that support community growth.

🌍 Phase 3: Community Growth & Partnerships

Expand the community, welcome contributors, and build partnerships that strengthen the project.

🪙 Phase 4: Future Digital Development

Prepare future steps toward a complete digital ecosystem with clear plans and community involvement.

🎁 Phase 5: Community Participation

Support fair participation and recognize users and contributors who help build SyriaCoin.
