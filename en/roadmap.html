SyriaCoin Roadmap

🚀 Phase 1: Points System

Launch the SyriaCoin platform, build the first community, and introduce the rewards and points system.

📱 Phase 2: Application Development

Develop a mobile application and improve the user experience with new features and activities.

🌍 Phase 3: Community Growth

Expand the community, introduce tasks and contribution programs, and create new partnerships.

🪙 Phase 4: Token Development

Prepare the transition from points to a digital token system with clear rules, transparency, and community participation.

🎁 Phase 5: Distribution & Ecosystem Expansion

Launch community distribution programs, support contributors, and continue developing the SyriaCoin ecosystem.
