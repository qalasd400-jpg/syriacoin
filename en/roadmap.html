SyriaCoin Roadmap

🚀 Phase 1: Points System

Launch the SyriaCoin platform with a points and rewards system to build the first community and create user participation.

📱 Phase 2: Application Development

Develop the platform experience, improve features, and create tools that support the community.

🌍 Phase 3: Community Growth

Expand the community, welcome contributors, and build partnerships to strengthen the project.

🪙 Phase 4: Digital Token Development

Prepare future steps toward a digital token ecosystem with clear rules, transparency, and community involvement.

🎁 Phase 5: Community Distribution

Support fair participation and community growth through planned distribution programs.
