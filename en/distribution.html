SyriaCoin Distribution

🌍 Community & Contributors

80%

The largest share is dedicated to the community, contributors, and participation programs. Our goal is to make the community the foundation of success.

🚀 Development, Listings & Partnerships

8%

Allocated for project development, infrastructure, future listings, and strategic partnerships.

🏆 Top 150 Users

2%

Dedicated to recognizing the most active and valuable users who contribute to SyriaCoin growth.

👥 Development Team

10%

Allocated to the development team and project contributors, with a clear locking system to protect community trust.

🔒 Team Token Lock

The team allocation will be locked and cannot be used or sold except according to official announced rules.

Only 2% will be unlocked every 4 months, with official announcements through project channels.

🗳️ Community Decision

Even after unlocking the allowed percentage, the team cannot sell or transfer tokens without a public vote.

The community has priority in voting, with participation from the full team to ensure fairness.

🤝 Official Commitment

The team and project manager will sign an official written agreement to not dispose of any allocation without community involvement.

SyriaCoin aims to build one strong family: the team and the community together.
