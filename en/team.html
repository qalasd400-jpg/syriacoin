SyriaCoin Team

🤝 Team Commitment

The SyriaCoin team is committed to building a project based on transparency, credibility, and cooperation with the community.

🔒 Protecting Community Trust

Clear rules will be established to protect the community's rights and ensure that important decisions are handled responsibly.

📜 Official Agreement

The team and project manager will sign an official written agreement to not dispose of any allocation or project rights without following the announced community process.

🗳️ Voting and Decisions

Important decisions related to the team allocation or project future will follow a clear and announced process.

The community has priority in expressing its opinion, with team participation to maintain balance and fairness.

❤️ One SyriaCoin Family

The SyriaCoin team and community work together as one family to overcome challenges and build a strong sustainable future.
